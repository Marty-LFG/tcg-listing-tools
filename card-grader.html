Card Pre-Grader

Photograph or scan a card → measured centering + an AI condition pass → a predicted grade per company, value-at-grade, and a "worth grading?" verdict.

⚠ Estimate, not a grade. This screens cards — it's far better at confirming a card is not a 10 than proving it is one. Surface flaws & the back are under-seen from photos; it does not authenticate. Treat every number as a guide, especially at the 9-vs-10 line.
